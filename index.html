How it works

Three simple steps to get the right help to the right place

1

NGOs report needs

Local NGOs and social groups log community problems — food shortages, medical needs, disaster relief — with location and urgency level.

2

Volunteers register skills

Community volunteers sign up with their skills, location, and availability so they can be matched to tasks that fit them best.

3

AI finds the best match

Our AI engine analyses all registered volunteers and ranks the top matches for each need — explaining exactly why each person is the right fit.
